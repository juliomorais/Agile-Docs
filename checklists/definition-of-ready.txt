Definition of Ready (DoR) Checklist

A user story is considered Ready when:
- The story is clearly written and understandable by the team
- Acceptance criteria are defined
- Dependencies are identified and managed
- The story is estimated in story points
- The story has business value defined
- The priority is set in the backlog
- The team agrees it can be completed within a sprint

Purpose: Ensure that backlog items are well-prepared before entering a sprint.
